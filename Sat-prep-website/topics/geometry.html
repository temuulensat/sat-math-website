Lesson 3: Coordinate Geometry (Slope)

Goal: Find slope and interpret lines.

Slope formula: (y₂−y₁)/(x₂−x₁)

Example: (1,2) to (5,10): (10−2)/(5−1)=8/4=2

Quick Practice: Slope from (2,3) to (6,11)?

Answer Key: (11−3)/(6−2)=8/4=2
