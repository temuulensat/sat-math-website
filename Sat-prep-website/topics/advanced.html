Lesson 1: Exponents & Radicals

Goal: Simplify expressions using exponent rules and square roots.

  • x²·x³ = x⁵ (add exponents)
  • 2⁴ = 16
  • √81 = 9

Example: If 2^x = 16, x = 4.

Quick Practice: (1) 2^5 = ? (2) √49 = ?

Answer Key: (1) 32 (2) 7

Lesson 2: Quadratics (Factoring)

Goal: Solve quadratic equations by factoring.

Example: x² + x − 6 = 0 → (x+3)(x−2)=0 → x=−3 or x=2

Quick Practice: x² − 9 = 0

Answer Key: x=3 or x=−3
